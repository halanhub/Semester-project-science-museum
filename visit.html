Tips for Your Visit

Best Time to Visit

Weekday mornings are typically less crowded. Plan to spend at least 2-3 hours to explore all exhibits.

Photography

Photography is allowed in most areas. Flash photography is prohibited in certain exhibits.

What to Wear

Comfortable shoes are recommended as there's a lot of walking. The museum is climate-controlled.
