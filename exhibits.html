Our Exhibits

Explore our interactive and educational exhibits

Robotics Lab

Experience the future of automation in our state-of-the-art robotics lab. Interact with various robots and learn about artificial intelligence and machine learning.

  • Interactive Robot Demonstrations
  • Programming Workshops
  • AI Learning Station

Space Exploration

Journey through the cosmos in our immersive space exhibit. Learn about planets, stars, and the latest discoveries in astronomy.

  • Planetarium Shows
  • Space Mission Simulator
  • Meteorite Collection

Biology World

Discover the fascinating world of living organisms. From microscopic life to complex ecosystems, explore the diversity of life on Earth.

  • Microscope Station
  • Ecosystem Displays
  • DNA Learning Center

Evolution Exploaration

Evolution Trace the origins of life on Earth in our dynamic evolution exhibit. Discover how natural selection, genetic variation, and environmental pressures have shaped species over billions of years.

  • Evolutionary Timeline
  • Fossil Dig Site
  • Interactive DNA Lab

Ecology

Delve into the complex web of life in our immersive ecology exhibit. Explore how organisms interact within ecosystems, the importance of biodiversity, and the impact of human activities on environmental balance.

  • Rainforest Canopy Walk
  • Pollinator Meadow
  • Interactive Food Web Station
